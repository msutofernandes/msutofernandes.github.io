Chapter 11 - Usability as common courtesy

If you make a user feel bad they might not only leave your Web site but think less of your company and savage you on social media.

Avoid:

  • Hiding information, punishing the user for not doing things correctly, asking for unnecessary information, loading needless pictures or logos, amateur looking Web site.

Seek:

  • Know what the user wants to know and make them obvious and easy to access, show what users want to know, save steps whenever possible, provide printer-friendly pages, make an obvious way to recover from an error, apologize when you canâ€™t do something.
