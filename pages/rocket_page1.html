Chapter 1 – DIY Usability Testing

What is usability testing?

Watching users try to use your product for the purpose of making it easier to use or proving that it is easy to use.

There are two categories:

  • Quantitative: to prove that something is better by measuring and being rigorous with it.
  • Qualitative: get insights to improve what you’re building.

The difference between usability testing and web analytics is that analytics can tell you in details what people are doing on your website but it can’t tell you why. Usability testing helps you understand why people are doing things.
